Patchwork Sunflower Jeans

Materials: Denim, Cotton, Polyester

Inspiration: This piece was inspired by my dream of ending climate change, so I decided to upcycle clothes as the medium. In addition, the flower design was influenced by “The Time Machine,” by H.G. Wells. In the novel, flowers represent a multitude of things such as kindness and empathy persisting despite the fact that time has passed drastically. This felt like a perfect motif to sew into this new pair of jeans as I hope people will approach our planet with kindness and empathy.
